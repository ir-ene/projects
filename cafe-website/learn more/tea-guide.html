Tea Guide

  • To make a stronger tea, don't steep for longer. Instead, add more tea leaves or tea bags.
  • The general rule is to use one teaspoon for every 8oz cup of water.
  • Spring water is ideal for brewing, but not necessary. Cold water is recommended.
  • Water that has reached a rolling boil will result in a flat-tasting cup of tea.
  • Many teas can actually be steeped multiple times. This commonly includes green, ooolong, pu-erh and white teas.
